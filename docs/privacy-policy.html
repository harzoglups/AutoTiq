1. Introduction

AutoTiq is an open-source Android application designed to automatically launch the Fairtiq app when you approach your saved transit points (train stations, bus stops, etc.). This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Data Collected

All data collected by AutoTiq is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

The application collects and stores the following data locally:

  • GPS Location: Your current location is used to detect when you approach saved transit points. Location data is processed locally and is never sent to any server.
  • Saved Transit Points: The geographic coordinates, names, and active time windows of your saved transit points are stored in a local database (Room DB).
  • Proximity States: The application tracks whether you are inside or outside each proximity zone to avoid repeated triggers (anti-spam mechanism).
  • User Settings: Your preferences (check interval, proximity distance, vibration count, active days, map layer) are stored locally using Android DataStore.

What we DO NOT collect:

  • No user accounts or authentication
  • No personal identification information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising data
  • No cloud synchronization

3. Permissions Used

AutoTiq requires the following Android permissions to function properly:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: Required to detect your precise location for proximity detection.
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Required for approximate location detection.
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: Required to monitor your location in the background and automatically launch Fairtiq when you approach a saved point. This is the core functionality of the app.
  • VIBRATE: Used to vibrate your phone when entering a transit zone.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Used to display notifications when Fairtiq is launched.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Used to restart background tracking after device reboot (if enabled).
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: Used to check if the Fairtiq app is installed on your device.
  • INTERNET: Used only to download map tiles from OpenStreetMap. No personal data is transmitted.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check network availability for map tile downloads.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database: Stores your saved transit points and proximity states.
  • DataStore: Stores your user preferences and settings.
  • OSMDroid Cache: Stores downloaded map tiles for offline use.

No cloud backup, no external servers, no data transmission.

If you uninstall the app, all data is automatically deleted from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

AutoTiq uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap: Map tiles are downloaded from OpenStreetMap servers to display the map interface. This is standard usage and does not transmit any personal data. Map data ¬© OpenStreetMap contributors, available under the Open Database License (ODbL).
  • Google Play Services Location: Standard Android API used to access GPS location. No data is sent to Google beyond standard Android system usage.

What we DO NOT use:

  • No analytics services (Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking or profiling services

6. Fairtiq Integration

AutoTiq is designed to launch the Fairtiq application automatically when you enter a transit zone. However:

  • AutoTiq does NOT interact with Fairtiq's data or API
  • AutoTiq does NOT read, modify, or transmit any data from the Fairtiq app
  • AutoTiq simply launches Fairtiq using a standard Android Intent
  • You must manually validate your journey in the Fairtiq app after it launches
  • AutoTiq does NOT replace Fairtiq or automate the check-in process

AutoTiq is simply a reminder tool to help you not forget to launch Fairtiq.

7. Background Location Usage

AutoTiq uses background location access to monitor your position and detect when you approach saved transit points. This is the core functionality of the app.

How background location is used:

  • A WorkManager task checks your location at regular intervals (configurable, default 60 seconds)
  • Location checks only occur on active days you've configured (e.g., Monday-Friday)
  • Location checks only occur within time windows you've configured for each point (e.g., 7am-9am)
  • When you enter a proximity zone (default 100 meters), Fairtiq is launched automatically
  • All processing is done locally on your device
  • You can disable background tracking at any time in the app settings

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security mechanisms (encryption, lock screen, etc.)
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception or server breaches
  • Only you have access to your saved transit points and settings

10. Children's Privacy

AutoTiq does not specifically target children under 13 years of age. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

11. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all your saved points and settings within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any saved transit point at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Export: Currently not supported, but may be added in future versions
