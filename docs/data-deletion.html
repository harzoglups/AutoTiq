AutoTiq - Data Deletion Request

Important: AutoTiq stores all data locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server or cloud service.

How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data deletion. There are three methods available:

Method 1: Delete Individual Map Points (Recommended)

  1. Open the AutoTiq app
  2. View your saved map points on the map
  3. Tap and hold on any point you want to delete
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted

What gets deleted: The selected map point coordinates, radius, and associated proximity detection data.

Method 2: Clear All App Data via Android Settings

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps â†’ AutoTiq
  3. Tap Storage
  4. Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage
  5. Confirm the action

What gets deleted: All map points, proximity states, app settings, and preferences. The app will reset to its initial state.

Method 3: Uninstall the App

  1. Long-press the AutoTiq app icon
  2. Select Uninstall
  3. Confirm uninstallation

What gets deleted: All app data is automatically and permanently deleted from your device upon uninstallation.

What Data is Stored

AutoTiq stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Map Points: GPS coordinates and radius of locations you've saved
  • Proximity States: Whether you're inside or outside each saved zone
  • App Settings: Your preferences (check interval, vibration settings, etc.)
Data Retention: All data is stored indefinitely on your device until you manually delete it using one of the methods above. No automatic deletion occurs.

No Server Data to Delete

AutoTiq does not:

  • Send your data to any remote server
  • Store your data in the cloud
  • Create user accounts or profiles
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Backup your data externally

Therefore, there is no server-side data deletion request process. All data deletion happens directly on your device.
